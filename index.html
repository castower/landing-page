Flowers and bees: blossom friends
There are over 300,000 different species of flowering plants in the world!
Flowers can generate heat to attract pollinators.
Flowers can hear buzzing bees and emit a faint noise that is inaudible to humans.
Flowers are an essential food source for pollinators.
